Vision

We envision a world where starting a company feels more like launching a game—instant, fluid, and accessible to anyone with an idea.

Today's AI agents operate in isolation. They write code, answer questions, analyze data—but they don't coordinate. They don't scale into organizations. They don't run businesses.

We're building the layer that changes that: intelligent systems that don't just assist, but execute—managing operations, making decisions, and evolving autonomously.

The Shift

From tools to teammates

AI is transitioning from productivity software to operational infrastructure. The question is no longer what AI can help you do—it's what AI can do without you.

Coordination is the bottleneck

Individual agents are powerful. But businesses require orchestration—workflows, memory, context, and decisioning across time. That's the infrastructure we're building.

Introducing Cofounder

Cofounder is our first product: an AI system designed to operate as a founding team member—not just executing tasks, but managing entire business functions autonomously.

It's early. We're working with a small group of builders testing what's possible when AI doesn't just help—but runs the company alongside you.
